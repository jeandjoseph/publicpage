## Strategic Pillars & Roadmap
1. **Customer Experience & Loyalty 2.0** — Reduce friction and personalize at scale.
   - Launch wait-time SLAs and queue-busting (mobile POS; line heatmaps).
   - Fix price-scan parity via centralized price services and shelf-label audits.
   - Out-of-stock prevention using demand sensing and store-facing alerts.
   - Tiered perks: early access for Gold/Platinum; instant credits for service failures.
2. **Omnichannel Excellence** — Optimize BOPIS and Ship-from-Store.
   - Fast-pick zones in each format; 2-hr BOPIS SLA; curbside readiness.
   - Unified returns and exchanges across channels; digital receipts default.
3. **Assortment & Pricing** — Strengthen high-velocity categories.
   - Double down on Electronics and Bags & Travel with value bundles.
   - A/B test promo stack rules to cap dilution while protecting AOV.
4. **Ops & Workforce** — Empower associates and cut variability.
   - Staff scheduling tied to traffic forecasts; coaching on service cues.
   - Mystery shopper + NPS close-the-loop triggers.
5. **Data & AI Foundation** — Real-time decisions everywhere.
   - Computer vision for shelf accuracy; ML for demand and fraud; Copilot for store managers.

## Store-Format Plays
- **Flagship**: Experiential zones + events; attach accessories to Electronics.
- **Neighborhood**: Speed; front-end staffing; curated essentials.
- **Outlet**: Volume + value; clear price signage; simple promos.
- **Pop-up**: Seasonal hero SKUs; QR-enabled endless aisle.
- **Specialty**: Expert demos; appointment selling for high-ticket.
- **Warehouse Club**: Bundles; membership upsell at checkout.
